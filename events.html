What are the most popular events in Andalucia?

Event Date Description
Seville Fair April Every town has a designated fair or 'feria' week, which vary from place to place. See full list of dates here.
Semana Santa Malaga Easter week Every town celebrates Easter Week but in the bigger cities such as Malaga and Seville, the processions are more spectacular to watch.
Carnival Cadiz Shrove Tuesday week, February A way to let loose before the 40 days of Lent begin. Most towns put on a parade and dancing. The Cadiz carnival is the biggest, lasting 10 days.
Goyesca Fair Ronda First week of September Combining the town feria with their impressive bullring, Ronda has a week of activities ending with a spectacular bull fight on the Friday.
El Rocio Pilgrimage First week of September The Camino de Santiago of the South!
Night of San Juan 23rd June Celebrating the Summer Solstice, people congregate on the beaches and have dinner and a midnight dip to wash away evil spirits on the shortest night of the year.
Los Reyes Magos First week of January Something for the kids, if Father Christmas isn't enough, the three kings arrive to town in a parade.
