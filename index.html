Welcome to Andalucia!

Spanning more than 800km of beautiful coastline across eight diverse provinces, this Mediterranean region of Southern Spain is now one of the most popular European holiday destinations.

Roman and Arabic influences can be found throughout the region and filling it with impressive architecture but what Andalucia is also very famous for is it’s unique style of dancing, known as Flamenco.

Where is Andalucia?

The easiest way to get to Andalucia is by air, flying into the heart of the region’s Malaga International Airport (AGP). From there, you can catch a bus or a train out to the surrounding provinces or hire a car and go wherever the wind takes you!
