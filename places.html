What type of holiday are you looking for?

There is a lot more to Andalucia than just it's expansive coastline. Within the cities you can find a wealth of architectural wonders, and if you choose to head inland, there is a variety of national parks, including the Sierra Nevada mountains which take you into the clouds at over 3000m in altitude.
