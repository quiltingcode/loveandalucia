Best Things to Eat in Andalucia

Depending on your budget or how far you want to emerse yourself into true Andalucian culture will decide what type of food you choose to eat while you are there.

Michelin Starred Food

Andalucia currently boasts 18 Michelin starred restaurants, one with the full three stars called Aponiente located in Santa Maria in Marbella. Most are based along the extensive coastline, from Cadiz to Marbella to Almeria. Click here for further details.

Traditional Food

Food is very important in Andalucian culture so it's something not to be missed when passing through. Highlights of Andalucian locally made cuisine include:

  • Gazpacho
  • Tapas
  • Olive Oil
  • Honey
  • Iberian Pork

Beach Food

If you are in Andalucia during the summer months, a whole new restaurant experience is open to you. The Beach Chiringuito. These beachside restaurants are often right on the water's edge where you can enjoy a family-sized paella rice dish or sample the freshest shellfish or catch of the day.
